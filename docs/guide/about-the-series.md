# About the Video Series

This site is the written companion to **GDPR by Noor**, a short video series on YouTube hosted by Noor Maryam.

## About Noor

Noor Maryam is a privacy and GDPR specialist who helps founders, small teams, and growing companies make sense of the European privacy rules without drowning in legalese. Her work focuses on the parts that actually move the needle: clear notices, real reasons for keeping data, sensible vendor contracts, and a calm plan for the day something goes wrong.

Find Noor:

- YouTube: <a href="https://www.youtube.com/@GDPRbyN00R" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">@GDPRbyN00R</a>
- Website: <a href="https://nmaryam.com" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">nmaryam.com</a>

## Why a 60-minute series?

The full GDPR is 99 articles and 173 recitals. Most teams do not need to read all of it, but everyone who handles customer info should know the spine of the law. The series is built around exactly that: ten short videos, one per topic, that fit into a lunch break. The written pages on this site go deeper when you want detail or when you need to show a teammate the exact article.

## How to get the most out of it

Watch each video first. Then if you want the detail, scroll. Each page links the law itself, so you can always read the original text in 24 EU languages on EUR-Lex.

If you get stuck or have a question about your own setup, the easiest thing is to message Noor directly through her channel or her site.
